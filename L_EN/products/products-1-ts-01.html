SPRAY DRYER
 
 
Our Technological Strength

Our spray dryer uses hot air to continuously and instantaneously dry plasma into powder, and collects the raw material after drying with air transport.

Using a special nozzle (pressure or twin-fluid type) or a high-speed rotating disc (centrifugal type), the spray dryer pellets slurry to increases surface area while applying continuous hot air for instant drying. Applying spray granulation technology to heat-sensitive materials rarely causes material deterioration, and omits the tedious procedures of concentration, filtering, grinding, grading.

The powder is spherical for great mobility, easily soluble in water for the food industry, and produces products with high density and consistency for the electronic materials, powder metallurgy and precision ceramics industries.
